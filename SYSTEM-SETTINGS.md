# System settings

> [!NOTE]
> What system settings do I update when setting up a new Mac for the first time?

## General

### About

- Give the machine a unique name.

### AutoFill & Passwords

- Turn everything off.

### Date & Time

- **Enable:**
  - 24-hour time.

### Language & Region

- **Date Format:** `YYYY/M/D`

## Menu Bar

- **Focus:** Always show
- **Battery:** `Battery Options...`, enable `Show Percentage`
- **Enable:**
  - Bluetooth
- **Disable:**
  - Screen Mirroring
  - Display
  - Sound
  - Now Playing

## Desktop & Dock

### Dock

- Resize to whatever looks compact but still accessible.
- **Disable:**
  - `Show suggested and recent apps in Dock`.

### Widgets

- **Disable:**
  - `Show Widgets On Desktop`.

## Spotlight

- **Disable:**
  - `Show Related Content`
  - `Help Apple Improve Search`
- **Disable in `Results from Apps`:**
  - Books
  - Calculator
  - Dictionary
  - Mail
  - Messages
  - Notes
  - Phone
  - Podcasts
  - Reminders
  - Shortcuts
  - Tips
  - Voice Memos

## Focus

- Disable `Share across devices`.
